About Us


Side Lot Farms was officilally established in St. Louis, Missouri by Gabriel Keeeven in 2016. Growing up in the city, Gabe always imagined how the empty spaces could be used. His vision and exploration have blossomed into multiple entrepreneurial ventures.

Along with landscaping and lawn care services, Gabe also runs an urban farming project in the city of St. Louis under the Side Lot Farms name and logo. The project grows fully organic (mostly) heirloom varieties with no spraying.
